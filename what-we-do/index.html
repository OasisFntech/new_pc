Responsible investing

It is our responsibility as a member of the investment industry to ensure that our actions are geared towards a more sustainable future. By integrating environmental, social and governance (ESG) factors throughout our investment process, and by ensuring that these factors are managed over the lifetime of our investments, ICG can help to create more successful and sustainable businesses over the long‐term and generate enhanced value for our clients.
