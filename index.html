What we do

We provide flexible and sustainable solutions to both providers and users of capital

European Corporate

Flagship strategy with a 34-year track record

Strategic Equity

ICGSE is one of the largest investors globally that is focused exclusively on GP-led transactions

Credit

Investing in primary and secondary public credit markets

European Infrastructure

The European Infrastructure team looks to acquire a diversified sustainable portfolio of Core-+ mid-market infrastructure assets across Europe

European Mid-Market

The strategy is a replica of ICG’s European Corporate but targets smaller mid-market transactions

Life Sciences

The strategy is focused on creating and building life science companies addressing commercially attractive markets in diseases of high unmet medical needs

LP Secondaries

Secondary strategy focusing on LP-led transactions

North American Private Debt

Focuses on investing in primarily sponsor-backed North American middle-market companies

Real Estate

ICG Real Estate invests in real estate assets and platforms internationally
