Investing responsibly

ESG issues are an important driver of investment value and source of investment risk. By implementing responsible and sustainable business practices we can deliver both long-term value and attractive returns to our clients

Our ESG priorities

It is crucial for us to understand, prioritise and report on the issues that matter most to our business and our stakeholders. Focusing on our most material issues will underpin our long-term success

Towards net zero

We are committed to support the goal of net zero GHG emissions by 2040 or sooner, in line with global efforts to limit warming to 1.5°C

Where we operate

We have built a global business serving a blue-chip institutional and wealth management client base from 15 offices in 12 countries across Europe, the Americas and Asia
